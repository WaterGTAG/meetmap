Meetmap Privacy Policy

Updated August 15, 2025
Meetmap is an app that allows people to easily manage their status (a set of buttons used to represent your real-life status for a specific event) and view others’ statuses within a meetup (a page for events where people can view everyone’s statuses and change their own).
We try our best to make a fun and easy-to-use app for meetups—big or small—to manage their attendees.
By creating a Meetmap account, you agree to this privacy policy.

A privacy policy explains how & why we use your data.

What We Collect

At Meetmap, we only collect data that's necessary to make the app function. We collect account information (the stuff you submit when you create an account) and device information (country, phone model, operating system).

Who We Share Your Data With

Meetmap does not sell your data. We share your data with other apps that provide hosting for our services. Here's a list of which tools & providers we use to make Meetmap:

• FlutterFlow - The no-code app builder we use to make Meetmap.
• Webstudio.is - The website builder used to make the website you're on right now.
• Firebase - The platform that holds the Meetmap user database, hosted by Google.
• WaterGTAG.com - The founder's merch shop that has Meetmap's merch collection, hosted by Fourthwall.

Children's Privacy

We do not willingly collect data of children under 13.

Changes to Terms

If there are any changes to Meetmap’s Terms of Use or Privacy Policy, we will do our best to inform users by posting updates through the app and on our social media channels.

Contact Information

If you need to contact us, please send us a direct message on Instagram @meetmapme or email us at biz@watergtag.com.
